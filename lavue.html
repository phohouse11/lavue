About Lavue Nail Lounge

La Vue Nail Lounge, Inc. is built on a fairly new but proven successful nail salon concept worldwide called Nail Lounge or Nail Bar. A few years ago, the nail salon industry grew from an affordable service catering mostly to low and middle-income clients with very basic nail services to a high-end, luxury service to include many other pampering services including skin care, massage therapy, laser hair removing, micro blading, eyelash extensions and cosmetic tattooing to capture the high-end client segment in the market. Customers are being pampered with a full service wine lounge with beers, wines, cocktails and hors d'oeuvres while being service.

Why Lavue Nail Lounge? Lavue Nail Lounge offers a variety of first–class nail services that promotes comfort, beauty, well-being, and health. We use only the most trusted brands in the beauty industry. Our staffs were trained to follow a proper sanitization protocol that puts our customer’s cleanliness as the number one priority.

We are proud of our interiors.
