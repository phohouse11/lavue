About

The Pho House is a Vietnamese/French restaurant/cafe located in the proposed Shoppes at Twelve Oaks Plaza, Bradenton, Florida. The restaurant features a full menu of moderately priced "comfort" food influenced by Vietnamese and French cooking traditions, but based upon time honored recipes from around the world. The Pho House and cafe features a moderate Vietnamese menu with a full line of dessert Boba drinks, fruit drinks, soft drinks and French coffee. The Pho House Café will be owned and operated by The Pho House Inc., a Florida corporation managed by Christian Tran, a resident of Sarasota, Florida.

What is a bowl of Pho? Pho is served in a bowl with a specific cut of flat rice noodles in clear beef broth, with thin cuts of beef (steak, fatty flank, lean flank, brisket). Variations feature slow-cooked tendon, tripe, or meatballs in southern Vietnam.

Our Goals

The Pho House Café concept is built upon the success stories of Tampa and St Petersburg many Vietnamese family/casual dining and Vietnamese/French Café venues and Pho Restaurant. Located in the fastest growing and prestigious East Bradenton area near the Manatee Technical Institute, the Lake Erie College of Osteopathic Medicine (LECOM), the YMCA, the American Red Cross Lakewood Ranch Operation and Training Center, the Rosedale development, a community of more than 500 current single family homes and fast growing, the Mallory Park with 430 single family homes, the Savanna with 475 single family homes and the Echo Lakes development with 360 apartment complex, Special Tara and plenty of land for additional development. The Pho House Café will provide accessible and affordable high quality food, Boba- Tapioca, smoothies, and other fruit-based drinks products to the thousands of residents and students located within a five-mile radius. In time, The Pho House Café will establish itself as a "destination" of choice to the many residents of Lakewood Ranch as well as many students of the nearby institutions and tourists. The Pho House cafe's aim is simple. It will provide a completely sophisticated, sensual, yet casual French style dining experience for the many residents and visitors. It will be an affordable venture for patrons, one that will encourage them to return on many occasions. The menu will feature hearty fare of the type that is frequently out of the reach of the typical young professional...out of reach because time frequently prevents her/him from cooking hearty meals like those featured on our menu. Finally, and quite significantly, The Pho House Café will provide a much needed neighborhood based retail food operation that is currently unavailable to Lakewood Ranch neighborhood. It is projected that the business will generate 16 new jobs for the area.
